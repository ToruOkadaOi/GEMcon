To download the

GEM from [sysbiochalmers](https://github.com/SysBioChalmers/Human-GEM) - wget "https://github.com/SysBioChalmers/Human-GEM/blob/main/model/Human-GEM.xml"

enzyme constrained GEM from [sysbiochalmers](https://github.com/SysBioChalmers/ecModels) - wget "https://github.com/SysBioChalmers/ecModels/blob/main/ecHumanGEM/model/ecHumanGEM.xml"
